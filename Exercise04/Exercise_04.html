ABOUT ME

12.12.2013

Rosie Edwards is a freelance make up artist based in Sheffield, England. From natural to outlandish, Rosie prides herself on creating a cutting edge look to flatter each unique face. Rosie is a creative and accomplished artist with experience in many different aspects of makeup, including (but not limited to): print, runway, bridal, TV & film.
